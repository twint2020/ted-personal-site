I lead an operations and strategy team at Winc, a modern winery with a direct connection to the consumer, building a portfolio of brands for the next generation of wine drinkers. Previously, I managed operations at Tapcheck Inc., an early-stage FinTech startup. Let’s connect on LinkedIn.

I am active on the USC Young Alumni Council and Dornsife Alumni Advisory Council. I also serve as a LA Global Shaper for the World Economic Forum. I am interested in harnessing the transformative power of technology to solve global issues, investing in the mission of health & wellness, and reimagining the future of work.
